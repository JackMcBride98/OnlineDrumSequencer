How to Get Started

We recommend using this app in Google Chrome

  • Controls

    Use the Playplay_arrow, Pausepause and Stopstop button to loop through the drum pattern. The two sliders can be used to alter the bpm and swing of the loop.

  • Channels

    Clicking inside a square of the drum pattern will activate or deactivate it.
    The controls on the left of a channel allow you to adjust the volume, delete the channelclear or clear all activated squares in that channel.

  • Uploading and Adding Samples

    To upload a sample, simply click "Choose file", select a file from your computer, give it a name in the box to the right and press the upload button. publish
    Samples that aren't in the sequencer appear in the drop down list to the left of the upload section. Select samples from here and press the add buttonadd to add them to the sequencer.
